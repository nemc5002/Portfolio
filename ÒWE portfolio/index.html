About Me

Hi there, my name is Noah Murillo. As I mentioned before, I'm currently studying to become a designer through the Communication and Multimedia Design program at the Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen. I'm a 19 year old Dutch boy that has been happily living in a small apartment in the Korrewegwijk neighborhood of Groningen for the better part of a year.

I have a broad range of interests that fuel both my creativity and curiosity. I love gaming, reading, writing, making music, photography, and, of course, design. These hobbies not only bring me joy but also inspire my work and the way I think about visual communication. The only thing I haven't quite fallen in love with (yet) is coding and programming.

Alongside my studies, I work part-time at IKEA as a kitchen designer. It's a role that allows me to combine creativity with functionality. I really enjoy helping people bring their interior ideas to life and help them realise their ideal kitchen.

I come from a family with divorced parents, a Dutch mother and a Peruvian father. I have one younger brother and two younger sisters. I also regularly visit my grandparents, with whom I share a close bond. Family is important to me.

Outside of work and study, I'm fortunate to be surrounded by a great group of friends. Whether we're playing board games or online games, heading into town for dinner or drinks, traveling together, or simply having deep, meaningful conversations, I always have a great time with them. I really value the people around me and the memories we create. I couldn't wish for better friends.
